I helped train and scale CSM's [Implicit Shape Foundation Model](https://x.com/CSM_ai/status/1852131242075910503), a 3D-native latent diffusion-Transformer model that produces 3D occupancy fields from single image in <1 minute, and its pre- and post-training data infrastructure. My work helped CSM land #1 on the [3D Arena Leaderboard](https://huggingface.co/spaces/dylanebert/3d-arena) and close initial enterprise contracts.
